# User Story Summary

**Profile**: Tester 01 works independently as an LLM solution tester, responsible for validating AI-related tools and platforms. Their job involves thorough scenario testing — including edge cases — to ensure each solution performs reliably. Tester 01 is deeply focused on clear documentation, using MS Word to create structured reports for developers, often organized in table or checklist format.

**Key Details**:

- **Daily Activities**: Testing LLM tools and prompts, analyzing outcomes, writing structured reports
- **Goals/Motivations**: Ensure reliability of AI tools; uncover edge cases not covered by predefined checklists
- **Tools & Platforms**: MS Word, web-based AI platforms
- **Communication Style**: Clear, formal, structured
- **Preferred Output Format**: Tables and checklists, easy to copy/paste
- **Traits to Emphasize**: Precision, systematic reasoning, attention to detail
- **Contextual Notes**: Dislikes casual tone and emoticons; not currently pursuing automation
